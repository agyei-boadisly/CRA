Introduction

Objective

My Approach

Report

The financial institution provides an average credit of $14,755 and between the range of $500 to $35,000 to customers. Risky loans as defined by the institution make up about 5.3% of loans given and non risky loans make up about 94.7% of the total loans given. Customers within loans grades B, C, and D should be scrutinized properly before verifying their loans, since, they are highly expected to default on their loan repayment.

Also, customers generally prefer short term loans than long term loans. There is also a high incidence of loans used for the purpose of debt and credit card consolidation. This shows clearly that, borrowers already have ongoing loans, then they desperately take loans even at high interest rates to clear other ongoing loans at different financial institutions.

Debt consolidation loans are taken when one is unable to pay for an ongoing loan, hence acquiring these types of loans at high interest rates becomes a hassle to repay.
